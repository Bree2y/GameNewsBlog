# Dive Into the Sands: Dune: Awakening Offers Free Trial and a Spicy Discount!

Are you ready to immerse yourself in the sand-swept landscapes of Arrakis? The time has never been better to answer the call of the desert, as Funcom's epic online adventure, **Dune: Awakening**, is rolling out a tantalizing **free trial** that runs until November 27. For those eager to take their exploration to new heights, a cool **25% discount** sweetens the deal for anyone who decides to purchase the game by December 1.

### Discover the Dunes for Free!

During this limited-time free trial, you'll have the opportunity to "taste the spice" and navigate the expansive Hagga Basin map alongside the Fremen warriors. Whether you're on a solo march or uniting with other players from around the globe, you'll have a solid 10 hours to carve your path through the desert. The best part? Any progress you make in this adventure will be seamlessly transferred if you decide to purchase the full game, ensuring that your journey is uninterrupted and richer than ever.

### Beyond the Trial: Exciting Updates Ahead

As if the trial and discount weren't enticing enough, there's more exciting news on the horizon. Funcom is diligently working on an ambitious roadmap of both free and paid DLCs, promising to deliver an ever-evolving experience in the sands of Arrakis. Up next is **Chapter 3**, a free update set to launch between January 2026 and March 2026, which will feature a reimagined endgame experience. 

Although specifics on **Chapter 4** remain shrouded in mystery, dropping somewhere between April 2026 and June 2026, Funcom has been proactive in implementing fan-favorite changes already. Expect enhancements like improved endgame dynamics, enriched player logging, new loot treasures in the deep desert, and a nifty tool to locate or salvage those elusive lost vehicles.

### Don’t Miss Out!

Whether you're a Dune disciple or a newcomer to the sands, now is the perfect chance to see what the fuss is all about. With thrilling updates and such an engaging trial period, who can resist the allure of Arrakis? Grab your stillsuit, rally your fellow adventurers, and dive into the world of **Dune: Awakening**. Remember, the spice must flow—and so should your adventures!
