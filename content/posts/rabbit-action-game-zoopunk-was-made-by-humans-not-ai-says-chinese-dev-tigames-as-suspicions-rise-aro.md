# **Unmasking Zoopunk: TiGames Sets the Record Straight on AI Controversy**

In a surprising twist that has the gaming community buzzing, the creators of the eagerly anticipated *Zoopunk* have found themselves clearing up a swirling storm of speculation. Following its dazzling appearance at Microsoft's Xbox Game Preview Event, suspicions have arisen suggesting that the unique, rabbit-centered action game is the product of generative AI. TiGames, the ingenious minds behind this spellbinding title, have taken a firm stand to dispel these rumors and assert the human creativity behind every aspect of their work.

The curious mix of action, whimsy, and engaging gameplay mechanics showcased in *Zoopunk* left audiences eager to dive into this adventurous world. Naturally, when such groundbreaking content emerges, it's easy to wonder about the tools and processes behind its creation. However, the team at TiGames has made it clear: *Zoopunk* is the brainchild of human ingenuity, not artificial intelligence.

TiGames, a renowned Chinese development studio, has openly defended its creative process. They've been vocal in sharing that the game’s artistic flair and innovative spirit stem from a talented team dedicated to crafting experiences that push the boundaries of gaming narratives and mechanics. The rabbit protagonist, a delightful yet formidable force within the game, is a testament to the studio’s commitment to delivering a product both original and entertaining.

With its eye-catching graphics and immersive world showcased at the Xbox event, it's understandable why *Zoopunk* caught the attention of both fans and industry insiders. Yet, in these times where AI-driven tools are reshaping various creative fields, the distinction between man-made and machine-made becomes critical. TiGames’ statement serves not only to clarify their position but also to highlight the authenticity and care poured into *Zoopunk's* development.
