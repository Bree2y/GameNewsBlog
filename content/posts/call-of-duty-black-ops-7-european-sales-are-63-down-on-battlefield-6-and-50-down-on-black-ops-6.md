# **Call of Duty Stumbles: Black Ops 7's Rough Launch in Europe**

The gaming universe is buzzing with a recent shocker: **Call of Duty: Black Ops 7** has taken an unexpected hit in Europe, and the figures are stirring conversations worldwide. To compound the surprise, Black Ops 7 also fell short by **50%** when stacked against its predecessor, Black Ops 6.
